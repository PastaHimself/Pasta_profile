About Me

I'm Pasta, a Minecraft Bedrock addon creator who likes turning ideas into game-ready experiences. I combine custom models, polished textures, and scripting to create content that looks good, feels smooth, and stands out without losing the Minecraft feel.

What I Specialize In

My work focuses on the parts that make an addon feel complete, from visual style to gameplay logic.

▣

3D Modeling

I design custom models for mobs, items, and blocks with clean shapes, readable forms, and a style that fits Bedrock content.

✦

Texturing

I create textures that feel polished and distinct while still matching the visual language players expect from Minecraft.

⌘

Scripting

I build the gameplay logic behind addons so custom features, interactions, and mechanics work smoothly in-game.

My Workflow

I usually take a project from concept to final polish through a simple production flow.

1

Concept

I start with the idea, theme, and how the addon should feel in gameplay.

2

Model

I build the 3D assets and make sure the forms are clean and readable.

3

Texture

I texture the assets to match the style and make the visuals feel complete.

4

Script

I add behavior, mechanics, and polish so everything works smoothly in Bedrock.
